About AskMe.ai

AskMe.ai is a futuristic AI-powered chat assistant designed to redefine the way you interact with technology. Whether you're looking for fast answers, inspiration, or productivity tools, AskMe.ai offers an immersive, sci-fi themed interface that feels intuitive and engaging.

Our goal is to create a minimalist yet powerful platform that balances aesthetic design with real functionality. We’ve carefully crafted AskMe.ai to perform beautifully on both mobile and desktop — with smooth animations, responsive layout, and an emphasis on clarity.

While the current version is a frontend demo, you can easily connect AskMe.ai to advanced language models or custom APIs. The code is lightweight, customizable, and open to experimentation — perfect for developers, designers, and tech enthusiasts.

AskMe.ai is not just a chatbot. It's a canvas for building your AI-driven ideas.
